# We write in plain English — always

**Decision:** All customer-facing copy uses plain English. No jargon, and no acronyms without explaining them first.

**Why:** Our customers aren't technical. Every time we leaned on jargon, confusion (and support tickets) went up. Plain English converts better and costs us less.

**So the AI knows:** never use jargon or unexplained acronyms in anything customer-facing.
