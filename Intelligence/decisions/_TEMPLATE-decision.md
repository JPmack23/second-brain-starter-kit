# Upsells default to annual, not monthly

**Decision:** All add-ons are sold annual-first. Monthly only if the client asks.

**Why:** Annual matches how the core licence is billed, and it makes revenue
predictable. Monthly looked friendlier but churned harder.

**So the AI knows:** never quote monthly pricing by default.
