# The Setup Interview — let AI build your second brain *with* you

Don't fill in the templates from a blank page. Let your AI interview you and do it for you.

**How:** open this folder in [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) and say *"run the interview in SETUP-INTERVIEW.md"* — or just paste the prompt below into any AI (ChatGPT, Claude) along with your `Context/about-me.md`.

It interviews you one question at a time, and it **starts with the why** — because if you can't answer that, the folders are pointless.

---

## THE PROMPT (paste this)

You're helping me set up my "second brain" — a structured memory you'll read at the start of every session so you always know who I am, what I'm working on, and how I want you to work.

Interview me **one question at a time.** Wait for each answer before the next. Keep it conversational, and when an answer is vague, dig deeper before moving on. Don't rush to the end.

Cover these, roughly in order:

**1. The why (most important — do not skip this).**
- What do you actually need a second brain *for*? What problem are you solving?
- How would your day change if your AI genuinely knew you and your work?
- *(If I can't answer this clearly, say so — because without the why, the rest is just tidy folders I'll abandon.)*

**2. Who I am.**
- What do I do — role, business or businesses, where I'm based?
- One line that captures me.

**3. My work.**
- What am I actually working on right now — the live projects and priorities?
- Who do I serve, and what do they need from me?

**4. How I want you to work with me.**
- Tone and format — direct or detailed? Bullets or prose? Any words or styles I hate?
- What should you **always** do? What should you **never** do?

**5. Facts you must never get wrong.**
- Names, numbers, spellings, rules that matter.

When we're done, write everything into `Context/about-me.md` using the structure already in that file (if you can't write files, just output the finished file for me to paste in). Then tell me, in two lines, how I keep this up to date so it gets smarter over time.

---

*That's it. Ten minutes of questions and your AI knows you better than most of the tools you pay for.*
